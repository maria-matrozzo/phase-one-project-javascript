Tarrasque

Description: The most dreaded monster on the Material Plane in D&D is none other than the ferocious Tarrasque. Another gargantuan monstrosity, these scaly bipeds stand fifty feet tall and seventy feet wide, towering almost every creature. The monster has the usual array of melee attacks and can inflict fright upon any creature fighting it. Similar to the Kraken, it also has the ability to swallow creatures whole. All of its attacks are powerful and its defense is very high with an armor class of 25. The Tarrasque has about 676 hit points.
